The Gap in Care

Excellent care exists.
Reassurance for families
doesn't always reach them.

When a parent moves into a retirement community or assisted living facility, families rarely stop worrying. They're busy professionals, often in different cities or time zones, trying to piece together how their loved one is really doing from occasional calls and fragmented updates.

At the same time, care facilities — who are already delivering excellent care — face mounting pressure to communicate regularly with families. It's time-consuming, hard to scale, and rarely standardised.

How It Works

A simple, consistent loop of
care and communication.

01

A SETUA caregiver engages with the resident

Our trained, verified caregiver meets with your loved one — building a genuine relationship through regular, empathetic interactions that go well beyond a checklist.

02

Wellbeing is observed and recorded holistically

Mood, social engagement, physical activity, emotional state, and behavioural changes are observed and structured into a clear, consistent wellbeing record — not just a list of tasks completed.

03

Families receive a warm, structured update

Family members receive a clear, readable summary of how their loved one is doing — via app or email. Written to reassure, not overwhelm. No need to call. No need to worry.

04

Facilities and families stay aligned

The facility's care team, family members, and SETUA all stay on the same page. Communication is consistent, transparent, and trusted — strengthening relationships on all sides.
